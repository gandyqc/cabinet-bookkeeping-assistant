Disconnect Cabinet Bookkeeping Assistant from your QuickBooks

Before you disconnect: if you are an active client of the firm, disconnecting will pause our bookkeeping services for your company. Please contact us before disconnecting so we can coordinate your files.

Option 1 — Disconnect directly from QuickBooks Online

This is the fastest method and is entirely under your control.

  1. Sign in to your QuickBooks Online company.
  2. Click the gear icon (top right) → Apps (or navigate to https://app.qbo.intuit.com/app/apphome).
  3. Open the My Apps tab.
  4. Find Cabinet Bookkeeping Assistant in the list of connected apps.
  5. Click the app's menu (⋯) and select Disconnect.
  6. Confirm the disconnection.

Intuit will immediately revoke the OAuth refresh token. The application will no longer be able to access your company's data.

Option 2 — Request disconnection through the firm

Send an email to Nbrazeau@mftcpa.com with the subject line "Disconnect QBO integration" and include your company name. We will:

  • disconnect the integration from our side within two (2) business days;
  • delete the stored OAuth tokens and cached data in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable record-retention rules;
  • confirm the disconnection by email.

What happens to your data after disconnection

When the application is disconnected:

  • OAuth refresh and access tokens are deleted from our systems within two (2) business days;
  • any cached copy of your QuickBooks data is purged, except for records that we are required to retain under our professional obligations (typically six (6) years for accounting work papers);
  • no further read or write operations can occur.

See the Privacy Policy (section 8 — "Your rights") for more details, including how to request deletion of records we are permitted to delete.

Need help?

If you have any trouble with the disconnection, email Nbrazeau@mftcpa.com and we will walk you through it.
