Cabinet Bookkeeping Assistant

An internal accounting tool developed and operated by Monette Fortin Tremblay inc., a Québec-based chartered professional accounting firm.

About

Cabinet Bookkeeping Assistant is a private, on-premise application used exclusively by the staff of Monette Fortin Tremblay inc. to perform bookkeeping services on behalf of the firm's clients. The application connects to QuickBooks Online via the Intuit Developer platform to read and post accounting data (vendors, customers, accounts, bills, invoices, reports) for clients who have explicitly authorized the firm to do so.

The application is not distributed to the public, is not available on any app marketplace, and is not accessible over the public internet. It runs on a local area network inside the firm's office.
