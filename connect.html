Connect or reconnect your QuickBooks company

Private application — firm clients only.
Cabinet Bookkeeping Assistant is an internal tool used exclusively by the authorized staff of Monette Fortin Tremblay inc. to serve its clients. It is not available to the general public and cannot be self-provisioned.

If you are an existing client of the firm

To authorize Cabinet Bookkeeping Assistant to access your QuickBooks Online company, or to re-authorize an expired connection, please contact your bookkeeping technician at the firm. We will:

  1. confirm the service engagement and the scope of access you wish to grant;
  2. send you a secure authorization link that triggers Intuit's OAuth 2.0 consent screen in your browser;
  3. after you click Connect on that screen, your QuickBooks company will be linked to the application and we will confirm activation by email.

You can disconnect the application at any time — see the Disconnect instructions.
